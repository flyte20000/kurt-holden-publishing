The Signature System — The Gold Flight Path

One continuous gold line ending with a refined Global 8000 silhouette. Not just in the logo — it appears everywhere across the brand. Think of it like the FedEx arrow: once you see it, you can't unsee it.

Brand Philosophy

The aircraft supports the brand. It does not become the brand. This brand communicates:

  • Executive Leadership
  • Aviation Excellence
  • Innovation & Precision
  • Trust & Timeless Design

The logo should feel equally at home beside Bombardier, NBAA, Apple, or a Fortune 500 executive presentation.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear Space

Minimum equals the height of the "K" in KURT. No text or graphics may enter this area.

Minimum Size

  • Digital: 120px wide
  • Print: 1.25 inches wide

Quality Standard — Before Approving Any Logo Application
Is the gold consistent?
Is the spacing correct?
Does it feel premium?
Does it look timeless?
Would it belong on an executive presentation or a business jet livery?

If the answer to any question is "no" — it is not approved for use.
